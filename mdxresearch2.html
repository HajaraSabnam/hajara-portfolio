Track Winner in MDX International Undergraduate Student Research Competition 2025


MDX International Undergraduate Student Research Competition 2025, held on January 16, 2025, at Middlesex University Dubai, brought together students from various disciplines to present innovative research under the theme "Human Centric Innovation in the Era of AI" exploring how emerging technologies can be harnessed to enhance human potential, ethicial decision-making and inclusive progress. The competition involved abstract submissions, followed by in-person presentations in the finale, where selected students showcased their research.

I was honored to be awarded Track Winner in Track 'Artificial Intelligence' for my research paper, "Tranquil Touch: Smart Gloves for Anxiety Management". Representing Amity University Dubai was an incredible experience, and I am grateful to the organizers and mentors for their support and insights.
